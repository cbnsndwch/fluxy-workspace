## Deep Research

now, let's start fleshing out a new app idea: Deep Sesearch (a management UI for deep research across the web, similar to ChatGPT's deep research feature)

- i add topics, with varying levels of detail
- you go scour the web, collect evidence, collate facts, record references, then finally write a report
  - the repo is veiwable in the app and can alspo be downloaded as a PDF for sharing
- you do research in teh background, piece by piece, as it may be too much to do in one go and it would block the chat (we only has this one channel right now)
- there should be a "keep research ongoing" toggle so I can mark specific ideas as long-term research directions. 
  - there should be a configurable "revisit" interval for each: every day, every week, twice a month, once a month, once a querter, once a year
  - when you wake up for your pulse you check which ad-hoc research tasks are still ongoing and which long-term research tasks are due for "revisiting" and schedule them for a refresh
  - when you "revisit" long term research tasks you always keep the previous data and reports for archival purposes and save the new data as a separate, self-contained bundle of information
