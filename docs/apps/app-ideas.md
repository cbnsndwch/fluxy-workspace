# App Ideas

A canvas for planning and tracking app ideas through a visual workflow.

## Stages

| Stage | Color | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| `idea` | Gray | Raw idea, not yet evaluated |
| `researching` | Blue | Actively investigating feasibility |
| `planned` | Purple | Decided to build, queued |
| `building` | Amber | Under active development |
| `live` | Green | Shipped and usable |
| `paused` | Orange | Deprioritized |
| `archived` | Red | Abandoned or superseded |

## Canvas controls

- **Drag** cards to reposition them (saved automatically)
- **Double-click** a card to open the edit modal
- **Drag from handles** (edges of cards) to create connections
- **Lock button** — prevents accidental card movement
- **Fit view** — centers all cards in the viewport

## Tips

- Use **groups** to cluster related ideas (e.g. "Q1 projects", "AI tools")
- Add **tags** for cross-cutting concerns (e.g. "auth", "payments", "mobile")
- Connections can have labels — useful for expressing dependencies
